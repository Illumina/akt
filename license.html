License

AKT is freely available under the GPL3 license. AKT is not commercially supported.

AKT relies on HTSlib and Eigen. Eigen is a header-only library for matrix algebra released under the MPL2 license. HTSlib is a library for efficently parsing vcf/bcf files released under the MIT/Expat License.
